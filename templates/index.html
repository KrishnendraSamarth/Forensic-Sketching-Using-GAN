Forensic Sketching using GAN is an AI-driven system designed to assist law enforcement agencies in identifying suspects based on hand-drawn sketches. By leveraging Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), the model learns to reconstruct realistic human faces from sketches, improving speed and accuracy in investigations.

This cutting-edge technology transforms traditional forensic sketching into a powerful digital tool, enabling investigators to generate photorealistic face reconstructions from witness descriptions and artist sketches in seconds.
